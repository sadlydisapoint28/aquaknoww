Place your fish images in this folder with the following names:

1. tilapia.jpg - For Tilapia fish
2. bangus.jpg - For Bangus (Milkfish)
3. lapu-lapu.jpg - For Lapu-Lapu (Grouper)
4. dalag.jpg - For Dalag (Snakehead)
5. tuna.jpg - For Tuna
6. mackerel.jpg - For Mackerel

You can download free images from websites like:
- Unsplash (https://unsplash.com)
- Pexels (https://pexels.com)
- Pixabay (https://pixabay.com)

Make sure to use high-quality images with a resolution of at least 800x600 pixels.
